ABOUT ME

Metallurgical Engineer

I am currently work FIFO as a Metallurgical Engineer at BHP NickelWest in the Processing Concentrator. Metallurgy excites me because I love the idea of being involved in a process that turns a natural resource into something useful. I have a range of experience including day to day Plant Production, Production Planning, Metallurgical Accounting, Metallurgical Lab Tests and Data Analytics. I have worked on projects which involve optimizing the plant to increase recovery and grade as well as being in the Process Commissioning team to commission the first Stack Cell (flotation cell) in Australia.
